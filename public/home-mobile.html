Join us

Jive is here to amplify your music.

Think of Jive as an electric amp. We want to use technology to get your music heard. We are a tool for musicians who have a message, sound, or unique style that they want people to hear. We are also a tool for music fans who want to discover their new favorite artist, or see their current one play live online. We are here to meet your music needs by providing an online platform for live music.

Sign up for our newsletter. Or else.

We want to empower artists.

At Jive, we see two main problems in today’s music industry: musicians aren’t being heard enough, and they aren’t being paid enough. We want to fix both of those problems.

‍With Jive, musicians will finally have a platform where they can control how much money they make, can connect directly with their fans, and can redefine not only the art of music but also the art of performance.

Music is an irreplaceable part of our lives, and we believe the artists who create it too often go underappreciated. Our mission is to make it possible for artists to make a living doing what they love: playing the music we can’t live without.
